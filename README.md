📌 Overview

Yojana Setu is an intelligent web platform designed to help individuals discover government schemes they are actually eligible for — in just under 2 minutes.

In India, thousands of central and state government schemes exist, but a large portion of the population remains unaware of them due to scattered information, complex eligibility criteria, and lack of accessibility.

Yojana Setu solves this problem by combining user profiling + AI-powered filtering to deliver personalized scheme recommendations, along with direct application links, deadlines, and step-by-step guidance.

❗ Problem Statement

Despite the availability of numerous welfare schemes, the major challenges faced are:

❌ Lack of awareness among citizens
❌ Complex and confusing eligibility criteria
❌ Information scattered across multiple websites
❌ Time-consuming manual search
❌ Low utilization of beneficial schemes

This results in millions of eligible individuals missing out on opportunities that could improve their lives.

💡 Our Solution

Yojana Setu simplifies the entire process by:

Collecting basic user details through a 2-minute form
Using AI (Gemini) to analyze eligibility
Filtering out irrelevant schemes
Displaying only accurate and personalized results
Providing:
✅ Direct application links
📅 Important deadlines
📝 Step-by-step application guidance
✨ Key Features
⚡ Quick 2-minute user profiling
🎯 Personalized scheme recommendations
🏛️ Coverage of both central and state government schemes
🔗 Direct application links for each scheme
📅 Deadline awareness
🪜 Step-by-step application instructions
💻 Clean and user-friendly interface
